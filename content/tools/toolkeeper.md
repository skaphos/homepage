Toolkeeper is a developer environment management utility published under Skaphos.

It is focused on interactive tool version updates and developer machine synchronization, pairing asdf-style version management with chezmoi-backed dotfile workflows.

## Positioning

Toolkeeper is a standalone utility rather than part of the core Skaphos fleet management system.
